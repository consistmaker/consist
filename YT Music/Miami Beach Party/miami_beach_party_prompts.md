```

[Verse]               16 bars  ~30s     — vokal masuk dengan beat ringan
[Build-up]            8 bars   ~15s     — tensi naik, snare drum roll cepat
[Pre-Drop]            4 bars   ~7s      — hening sebentar (silence gap)
[Vocal Chop Drop]     16 bars  ~30s     — klimaks instruemntal, bass terberat (Moombahton Drop)
[Verse 2]             16 bars  ~30s     — cool down, beat kembali normal
[Build-up / Drop 2]   24 bars  ~45s     — klimaks kedua yang lebih gila
[Outro Fade]          8 bars   ~15s     — transisi ke lagu berikutnya

KUNCI YANG SERING DIGUNAKAN (Untuk DJ Mixing harmonis):
Major Scales (G, D, C) for Summer/Daytime vibes.
Minor Scales (Am, Fm, Gm) for Nightclub/Deep Perreo vibes.
```

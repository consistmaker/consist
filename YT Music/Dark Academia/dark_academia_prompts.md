## ⚡ PERUBAHAN UTAMA v3.0

Setiap komposisi klasik sekarang punya struktur penuh **~4 menit** tanpa lirik, dirancang khusus untuk memandu AI Suno menavigasi dinamika emosi, dengan metatag seperti:
- `[Interlude]` — momen solo instrumen santai ("napas") sebelum klimaks
- `[Crescendo]` — eskalasi intensitas alat musik untuk menjaga penonton tetap fokus
- `[Grand Finale]` & `[Outro]` — penutup yang substansial dan megah
- Setiap section punya **tujuan musikal dan emosional** yang jelas untuk belajar/fokus.

```text
Durasi Target per Section (Orchestral/Classical Mode):
[Intro]           ~20 detik (Pengenalan tema)
[Adagio]          ~45 detik (Tempo lambat, stabil)
[Crescendo]       ~30 detik (Eskalasi)
[Forte]           ~45 detik (Klimaks pertama)
[Decrescendo]     ~20 detik (Penurunan energi)
[Interlude]       ~30 detik ← Kunci durasi & napas otak
[Grand Finale]    ~40 detik (Klimaks maksimal)
[Outro]           ~20 detik (Fade out natural)
─────────────────────────
Total             ~4 menit
```

## 🖥️ TUTORIAL SUNO v5.5 — STEP BY STEP

### STEP 1 — Pilih Mode
Pilih **Custom Mode**. Aktifkan tombol **INSTRUMENTAL** agar Suno tidak memasukkan penyanyi gaib.

### STEP 2 — Field "Style of Music"
Format: `[Genre Klasik] → [Mood/Aesthetic] → [Instrumen Lead] → [Tempo/Rhythm] → [Produksi]`
✅ **Benar:** `neoclassical dark academia, dramatic villain entry, aggressive slow cello, minor key violin ensemble, waltz rhythm, pure instrumental`
❌ **Salah:** `Mozart, Beethoven classical music for studying, scary, dark...` *(Bisa kena Copyright Strike!)*

### STEP 3 — Field "Lyrics" + Metatag Inline v5.5
Meski Instrumental, Anda **WAJIB** menaruh perintah struktur di kotak Lirik. 
Suno v5.5 sangat mematuhi arahan di dalam kurung siku `[ ... ]`.
Contoh Format:
```text
[NamaSection | instruksi musikal, instrumen masuk/keluar]
(Biarkan baris di bawahnya kosong atau tulis instruksi emosi)
```

### STEP 4 — Sweet Spot Setting (Sangat Kritis untuk Klasik)
- **WEIRDNESS: 15–25** (Wajib rendah! Musik klasik butuh harmoni yang stabil. Weirdness tinggi akan membuat biola fals dan melenceng).
- **STYLE INFLUENCE: 85–95** (Tahan tinggi agar mesin mematuhi instruksi orkestra/piano murni).

### PROMPT 01 — "The Royal Checkmate"
**Tema:** Ambisius, Gelap, Penjahat menyusun rencana.
**STYLE:** `neoclassical dark academia, dramatic villain entry, aggressive slow cello, minor key violin ensemble, waltz rhythm, pure instrumental`
**LYRICS/STRUCTURE:**
```text
[Intro | solo deep cello in minor key 4 bars, dark and brooding — tension builds slowly like a villain plotting]

[Adagio | violins enter softly, slow waltz rhythm established — cold and wealthy aesthetic, mysterious]

[Crescendo | tempo accelerates slightly, grand piano bass notes dropping heavily, strings pulling faster]

[Forte | full string ensemble pushing hard, dramatic tension peaking — the masterplan set in motion]

[Decrescendo | sweeping violin solo carries the emotion as other instruments pull back seamlessly]

[Interlude | sudden drop to solo piano, very slow and calculating — giving the listener space to breathe and focus]

[Grand Finale | full string orchestra returns with maximum aggression and speed, 3/4 waltz at its peak]

[Outro | slow single cello note fading into the dark, tension unresolved]
[End]
```

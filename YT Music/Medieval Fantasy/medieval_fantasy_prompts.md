```
Struktur standar AMBIENT MEDIEVAL (berbeda dari EDM/Flamenco):
[Atmospheric Intro]   8 bars  ~30s  — establishing the place
[Main Theme A]       16 bars  ~60s  — the primary melody
[Theme Development]  16 bars  ~60s  — gentle variation/development
[Solo Instrument]    14 bars  ~52s  — one instrument featured alone
[Bridge/Interlude]   12 bars  ~44s  — contrasting section
[Return Theme]       14 bars  ~52s  — familiar melody returns
[Peaceful Outro]      8 bars  ~30s  — gentle resolution
              ─────────────────────
TOTAL:               88 bars ~5:08 (di 70 BPM)

NOTE: Tidak ada BUILD UP dan DROP seperti EDM.
Dinamika hanya naik-turun secara halus (gentle swell).
```

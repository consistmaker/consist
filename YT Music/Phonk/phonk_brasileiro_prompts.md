### PROMPT 01 — "Montagem Estelar"
**Vibe:** Classic montagem — atmospheric intro membangun perlahan, drop yang keras

**STYLE:**
```
Brazilian phonk montagem, heavy 808 sub-bass, dark atmospheric synth,
pitched vocal chop texture no lyrics no singing, hard funk drum pattern,
3-4 minute extended instrumental, 150 BPM
```

**STRUCTURE:**
```
[Intro | 8 bars | eerie synth pad solo, distant reversed sample — listener pulled into darkness before rhythm even exists]

[Build 1 | 16 bars | 808 bass enters quietly bar 4, hihat pattern bar 6, pitched vocal chop drops bar 8, rising filter sweep — each 4 bars adds exactly one element, tension builds geometrically]

[Drop 1 | 24 bars | full 808 impact, funk drum pattern locks in hard — sub-bass physically felt, all elements arrive simultaneously, groove runs full 24 bars at maximum intensity]

[Break | 8 bars | bass drops completely — only hi-hat and atmospheric pad remain, reverb from previous section still decaying — eye of the storm, listener anticipates the return]

[Build 2 | 12 bars | 808 returns pitched lower, more ominous second build, new pitched vocal chop layer enters bar 8, filter rising faster than first build]

[Drop 2 | 24 bars | heavier than Drop 1 — additional bass layer, hi-hat doubles in density, distorted 808 tail, maximum sub-bass weight, groove locked in tighter than ever]

[Interlude | 20 bars | synth melody plays over the groove — ear gets melodic relief while body stays locked in the rhythm, the most "musical" moment of the track, melody remembered after the song ends]

[Final Drop | 20 bars | all elements from Drop 2 plus additional percussion layer — groove density at absolute maximum, the definitive statement of everything this track is]

[Outro | 8 bars | elements drop out one per 2 bars, last element: 808 tail decaying naturally to silence — satisfying resolution]
[End]
```
